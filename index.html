About

WHO WE ARE

Lendingcoinlist.com was started by two cryptocurrency entreprenuers Ryan and Justin. These two young investors have found recent success in various aspects of the cryptocurrency world and want to share it with more individuals. The more people invested in this movement the stronger it grows.


We aim to update this site as much as possible. Our vision is to showcase the best upcoming lending platform ICOs to choose from, eventually listing the non-lending platform ICOs as well. We are hoping to include some interviews with various ICO developers, in addition to other high quality information to present each reviewed ICO in an un-biased accurate manner.


This site will be continuously updated, as more functionality will be added across social media as well. The goal is to have our updates from the update log pushed to social posts in the near future, please follow our social presence at the bottom of the page.
